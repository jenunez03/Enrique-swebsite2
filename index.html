There's Always Time to Dream Bigger
Enrique's Gallery
Autumn leaves at Cheshire, CT
October, 2021

Autumn goes away in a blink of an eye. This painting shows one of my favorite places where I have experienced happiness and good people have surrounded me.

When I see this artwork, I teleport to that very place. Deep recollection, meditation, and peacefulness are what bring up this painting.
